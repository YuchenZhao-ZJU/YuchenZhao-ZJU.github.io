About Me

唐谈 is my Chinese name, in which the given name 谈 (tæn) means "talkative and outgoing". I am now a ZJU 100 young professor in the School of Art and Archeaology, Zhejiang University. I received my doctor degree from State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, where I worked with Prof. Yingcai Wu in the Interactive Data Group (ZJUIDG). My first job was a data scienctist worked with Prof. Yu Zheng at JD Intelligent Cities Research.
